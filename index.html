About InheritRight Properties

InheritRight Properties is a family-run real estate business dedicated to helping Kenyans navigate the complex process of buying, selling, and inheriting property. With over 10 years of experience in Naivasha and Nakuru, we've built our reputation on trust, integrity, and results.

We understand that property transactions can be stressful, which is why we handle every aspect of the process - from initial search to title transfer - with care and professionalism. Our team includes certified agents, lawyers, and surveyors to ensure all transactions are legal and above board.

Whether you're looking for land to build your dream home, rental income property, or need succession transfer support, we're here to guide you every step of the way.

Our Mission

Making property ownership accessible, understandable and achievable for every Kenyan family
